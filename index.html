Broadcast Talking Points

UNESCO Global Geopark

Torbay has a distinctive 400-million-year-old geological heritage. This provides our race venue with beautiful red sandstone cliffs and deep sheltered waters.

Dartmoor Microclimate

The vast peaks of Dartmoor to the northwest block heavy Atlantic gusts, keeping Torre Abbey Sands relatively smooth compared to open channels.

Birthplace of Agatha Christie

Torquay is the birthplace of Agatha Christie. Her famous writing landmarks are located just yards behind the beach promenade.
